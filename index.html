█ STRENGTH - DIRECTION PLANNING

Turntable and rotating printhead allows to build layers from any point in any time of printing process.

█ GEOMETRY

Features and shapes have the best geometry when printed vertically. With nonplanary all the features can be printed in their individual direction.
