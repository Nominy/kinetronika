New way for composites

We believe that future is built with composite materials - so we created a technology that can reveal their true potential. With speed and agility of additive technology, allowing to create parts with absolutely zero compromises in geometry and mechanical properties.

Rethink materials

Introducing Continouos Fiber Nonplanary Fabrication (CFNF) - an additive way to create complex parts with super properties, no need of custom molds and weeks of waiting. By using 5-axis continouos fiber reinforcing, we are implementing any-directional in-part anisotropy control for most strength in less mass.

Overcome limitations

Almost zero geometrical limitations compared to fabric or prepreg composite technologies - we made matrix/fiber coextrusion on 5-axis machine to fix material in almost any geometry and direction. Complex nonplanar parts are what we created technology for.

Also, our process results in most effective fiber layout - all the continouos fiber directions can be pre-engineered on slicing stage.

Fast composites workflow

Classic composite manufacturing workflow is expensive in time, money and labor work and requires custom molds for each part. With CFNF we are making composite parts production fully automated, simple and allowing to cut part production cycle from weeks to hours.
