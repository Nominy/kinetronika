Why nonplanary?

01

MORE STRENGTH

The strongest direction of the printed part is parallel printed layers. With nonplanary layers can be printed in multiple directions in one printing sequence.

STRENGTH - DIRECTION PLANNING
Turntable and rotating printhead allows to build layers from any point in any time of printing process.
Printhead builds reinforcement layers that are perpendicular to the main core layers.
02

BETTER GEOMETRY

Features and shapes have the best geometry when printed vertically.
With nonplanary, all the features can be printed in their individual direction.
